Realistic Elephant with Melted Wax on Canvas

September 14th 2019

I created this as a babyshower present for my cousin. I melted a ton of crayons over this canvas using a hairdryer, but I tapped over where the elephant would be. Then I began the lineart and shading for the elephant, im very proud of how it came out. Afterwards I cut our cardboard in the shape of the letters and boundaries of the elephant and superglued fabric on top to give it a nice fuzzy look.
