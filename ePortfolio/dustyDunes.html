Dusty Dunes Tycoon

Started Development Nov 2023

This is VR Mining Tycoon im currently developing within Unity. It started out as a project for one of my online classes but expanded as I found myself enjoying the process and adding more and more ideas. Im hoping to getsomewhere with this and have it on platforms such as steam and the oculus store.

The player is stationed within a minecart, and is able to unlock droppers that dispense unique types of gems. Using those gems the player can purchase speed or value upgraders for that dropper, as well as unlock new destinations to travel to via the minecart. There are planned to be plenty destinations to travese to, each prodiving the player a new dropper type or a fun side activity.

The side activitys are more hands on for the player, youre able to mine rocks which gives you gems and rock fragments in one of them and then test your skills with a shooting range within a valley.
