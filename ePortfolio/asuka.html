Asuka Langely Soryu Character Portrait

November 28th 2023

Asuka is one of my favorite characters from Neong Genesis Evangelion. Shes is a character who has more depth then most mainstream production shows today have in any of their characters, she resonates with many of the shows fans I included. I attempted to capture the many sides of her here, not just the good or the bad. As well as the many different character in her life.
