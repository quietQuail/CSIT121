Scatch Game - Pluckin Apples

December 2022

This is a game I created in scratch for an Online class I was taking. The premise is simple catch the falling apples with the basket, you move the basket by clicking anywhere on the screen. The basket can only go so far up however so you must time your catches so no apples hit the ground and rot.
