About Me!

❮ ❯

Hello! My name is Tyler Melka, welcome to my corner of the internet. This site doubles as an ePortfolio and an archive for any of my creations.

I enjoy creating art about things that have a close meaning to me. Im also an aspiring game developer currently learning unity and taking a VR Development Class online, which I will receive a cirtificate for. I genuinly enjoy building 3D enviorments that tell a story and let the player have fun. Im also currently attending college studying computer science and IT.

Life is a journey of one, I believe one of the best things we can do while were here is create. Find something you find beautiful with potential and build something you're proud to call your own
